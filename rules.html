RULES

Every hallway has a schedule of cleaning duties, one for hallway and one for the kitchen. The room responsible for kitchen and hallway duty changes every Friday. Cleaning check is every Tuesday. If the cleaning is not done, person responsible for it has to pay a fee.

The waste must be sorted - glass, plastic, paper and general waste. A more detailed description of the products that can be thrown into a given product group is on each garbage bin. If sorting is not followed, each floor will be fined.

Every hallway has a schedule of cleaning duties, one for hallway and one for the kitchen. The room responsible for kitchen and hallway duty changes every Friday. Cleaning check is every Tuesday. If the cleaning is not done, person responsible for it has to pay a fee.

The dorm residents can invite other people for time no longer than one week. Caretaker must be informed in such situation. Guests are obligated to follow the rules of residents of the dorm.

The events must be planned at least a month before the planned date - it also must be approved by the caretaker - send him an e-mail about the time of the event, in what room it will take place, and the estimated number of participants. Events cannot violence basic rules of the dorm.

The main cleaning takes part twice a year - in spring and winter. Everyone must take part in the cleaning, participating in this event is a mandatory. If cleaning doesn't get approved, the hallway gets fined. It is possible to get a second check, but it must be discussed and planned with the caretaker.

You can book a washing machine for a choosed date. You must contact a caretaker to do it. Write a number of washing machine, day and an hour when you plan to put your laundry on, as well as estimated time of laundry.
