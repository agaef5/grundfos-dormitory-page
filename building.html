BUILDING

Open since 2012

Grundfos dormitory gives students the possibility to be placed in the brand new part of Aarhus by the harbor.
The district will hold housing, industry, culture and recreational spaces and thereby create lungs for the city.

LOCATION
Grete Løchtes Gade 3, 8000 Aarhus
FACILITIES
Resident rooms

The residents are free to use it as pleased but they're also responsible for its cleaning according to the cleaning duty schedule. It is fully equipped, residents are responsible for any damaged or missing kitchen appliances.
